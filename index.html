A Culinary Lab in Orbit

Nebula & Knife is a chef-driven restaurant built around one idea: precision makes everything taste better. Our team combines modern cooking techniques with cosmic-inspired presentation, creating dishes that feel unexpected but still familiar and satisfying. Whether you're here for a full tasting journey or one standout entrée and a drink, you'll find flavors that are carefully balanced and plated with intention. The space is sleek, intimate, and designed to keep the focus on what matters—great food, great timing, and a night you'll remember.
